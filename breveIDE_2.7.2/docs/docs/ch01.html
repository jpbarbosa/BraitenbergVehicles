Chapter 1. Preface

This section contains some special notes about breve and about this documentation.

The Origin of breve

The simulation software described in this document, breve, was initiated by jon klein as a thesis project at Hampshire College and was developed further into a Master's thesis at Chalmers University. The software is actively being developed as a platform for a project building large scale simulations of evolutionary dynamics, but is also used for many other applications.
