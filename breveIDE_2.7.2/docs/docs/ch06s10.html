Sounds Included With breve

A few sounds are included in the breve distribution for use in any simulation. The are located in the classes directory, in a subdirectory called sounds.

The sounds provided are:

  • sounds/cat.wav

  • sounds/chimp.wav

  • sounds/dog_bark.wav

  • sounds/explosion.wav

  • sounds/laser_cannon.wav
